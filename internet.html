The Internet

The internet is a global network of connected computer systems. Computers are connected by cables, wireless, and satellite technologies.
The internet is used to transmit webpages, to facilitate the World Wide Web.

The World-Wide Web

The World Wide Web was proposed by Tim Berners-Lee in march 1989. He proposed a collection of hypertext pages which could be viewed by browsers.
Today, The WWW is used by billions of people to share information over the internet.
